Instruction Set

Clear, descriptive instructions are an integral part of communication in almost every single field and discipline. Whether it’s a customer trying to assemble their new couch or a corporate executive outlining new company policies and procedures, the quality of the instructions can decide whether the task at hand goes smoothly or ends in confusion. For English 202C, we were tasked with writing our own instruction set to get a better understanding of how to create clear, carefully explained instructions. I chose to create an instruction set describing how to set up a virtual machine. My instruction set begins with choosing a virtualization platform and guides a user all the way through completing the installation of their virtual machine.

Technical Document Analysis

Unlike most other forms of writing, technical communication’s purpose goes beyond just being read. A technical document is supposed to be useful to the reader in that it helps the reader solve problems. In this assignment for English 202C, we were tasked with analyzing how well a technical document implements effective communication practices. I chose to analyze the July 2018 jobs report from the Bureau of Labor Statistics. Although most people wouldn’t consider this an exciting document, it’s used by business people across the country and even the world to gauge how the United States economy is performing. This memo dives into where this document communicates effectively and areas where the communication practices could be improved.
